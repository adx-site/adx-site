APP Introduction：

FitPro is an app developed based on a Bluetooth wristband that uses Bluetooth 5.0 communication. It includes features such as sleep tracking, remote photography (taking photos through the wristband), alarm settings, sedentary reminders (setting reminder times to prevent users from sitting for too long), screen up, step counting, message push, Do Not Disturb mode, sleep monitoring, and finding the wristband. Users can use the app to intuitively understand their physical condition and exercise status.
