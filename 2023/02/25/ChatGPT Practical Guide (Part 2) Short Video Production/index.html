ChatGPT Practical Guide (Part 2): Short Video Production (Helping you quickly dominate Bilibili, TikTok, and WeChat Channels, haha)

Goal: Create a 3-minute short video (become a video content creator dominating Bilibili, TikTok, and WeChat Channels)

Resources:

1. Free images and videos: www.pexels.com
2. Video creation tool: https://www.veed.io/

Steps for Creation:

  1. Use ChatGPT to write the video script
  2. Use the AI tool Veed.io to create the video

1. Use ChatGPT to Write the Video Script

Key words: video, scripts

I want to create a 1-minute video about “what is the future of ChatGPT and how it can change our life,” can you create scripts for the video?

2. Use the AI Tool Veed.io to Create the Video

Website: https://www.veed.io/
Veed is a free-to-start video creation tool.

2.1 Convert text to speech
Translate the script into Chinese, click “Text To Speech,” and generate a 1-minute female voiceover.

2.2 Add background music
Use the built-in BGM (background music) of the video tool and insert it (lower the volume a bit).

2.3 Create a title
Add a cover and create a title, “The Future of ChatGPT?”

2.4 Add copyright-free images and videos
Free website (no registration needed): www.pexels.com
Download and add the images and videos.
