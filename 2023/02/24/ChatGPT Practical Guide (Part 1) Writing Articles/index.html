ChatGPT Practical Guide (Part 1): Writing Articles (Blogs, 10,000-word Papers, etc.)

With the advent of the AI era, I will share some practical guides on using ChatGPT for content creation and productivity.

Goal: Use ChatGPT to write an article of about 10,000 words (on the evolution of ChatGPT)

Steps for Creation:

  1. Write an outline;
  2. Write the detailed content based on the outline;
  3. Create an engaging title (viral title);
    Note: You can contact the editor to register an account for free.
    Special Note: Using English will be faster and more accurate; you can ask for a translation into Chinese afterward.

1. Writing an Outline

Different ways of asking will yield different results. Learning to ask questions effectively will improve the outcome.

For example:
1.1 To briefly discuss the evolution of ChatGPT, you can ask:
Can you provide an outline about “the evolution of ChatGPT”

1.2 For a long-form article or paper, you can ask: write an outline of “the evolution of ChatGPT” for a blog**

1.3 For a university thesis outline (more detailed), you can ask:
write an outline of “The ChatGPT Revolution: How AI Has Evolved” for a university thesis

2. Writing the Detailed Content Based on the Outline

Question: Can you provide an outline about “the evolution of ChatGPT”

2.1 Writing the first point

After receiving the outline, ask:
please write more about 1.

2.2 Writing the second point in an “expert” tone

please write detail on 2. as an AI expert

2.3 Specify the word count for the third point

write 3. as an essay, 500 words

3. Creating a Title

give me 5 viral name options for “the evolution of ChatGPT”

And just like that, an article about ChatGPT is born. The AI’s content generation and translation abilities are impressive!
